Tokenomics

Simple. Fixed. Transparent.

BGT has a hard-capped supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens. No minting after launch. No team unlock schedule to dump on you. Every token is accounted for.

🛍️ User Rewards Pool 60%
Distributed to users who scan groceries and earn BGT in the app. Released gradually over time.
🏦 Treasury 25%
Held in the Phantom treasury wallet for buybacks, partnerships, and ecosystem growth.
💧 Liquidity Pool 10%
Locked in the BGT/SOL Raydium CPMM pool to ensure smooth trading for all users.
🔥 Buyback & Burn 5%
Revenue from app subscriptions is used to buy BGT on the open market and burn it. Deflationary by design.
