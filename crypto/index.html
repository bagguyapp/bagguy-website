BAG token utility

BAG is designed as a fixed-supply SPL utility token around shopping activity, membership utility, and transparent treasury mechanics.

Premium rewards

BAG powers enhanced rewards for wallet-connected members who qualify through shopping activity.

Treasury support

Subscription revenue can support treasury reserves, reward pools, and ecosystem operations.

Buyback utility

Treasury mechanics can simulate and later support buyback and burn programs only after legal, security, and liquidity approvals.

Shopping activity

Users can earn rewards through scans, offers, purchases, meal planning, and verified engagement.
