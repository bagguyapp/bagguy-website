Core product

Built for shoppers who want savings without extra work.

Bag Guy turns the messy world of coupons, weekly ads, local offers, receipts, and meal planning into a simple scan-first workflow.

01

Universal Scan

Show one scan code at checkout, scan products, or scan receipts to match eligible savings and rewards.

02

Deals Marketplace

Browse local offers, manufacturer coupons, weekly grocery deals, household savings, and SNAP-friendly deals.

03

Bag Points + Store Credit

Earn Bag Points from eligible shopping actions and redeem rewards as Store Credit when available.

04

AI Meal Planner

Plan meals by budget, family size, cooking time, and local weekly ads. Add ingredients to your shopping list.

05

Shopping List

Add items manually, shop recipes, match coupons, check off items, and scan at checkout.

06

Shopper Share

Share lists, recipes, coupons, weekly deals, and budget baskets. Earn points when shared content helps others save.

Systems behind the app

Ready for real data, not just demo cards.

The current Bag Guy build includes backend-ready systems for product lookup, coupon matching, receipt rewards, provider onboarding, recipe recommendations, and safe internal operations tooling.

API

Coupon connector architecture

Built for Ibotta/IPN, Inmar, Eagle Eye, Kroger Products API, UPC lookup providers, retailer feeds, and admin-uploaded deals.

OCR

Receipt-safe reward flow

The mobile app uses a stable receipt contract with receipt IDs, idempotent rewards, and clear backend-down messaging.

AI

Recipe + product intelligence

Recipe recommendations, product metadata, UPC matching, coupon confidence, and fallback data are structured for real provider integrations.
