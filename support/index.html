Bag Guy support

Need help with savings, scanning, rewards, deals, or your account? We are here to help.

Contact support

Email bagguyappofficial@gmail.com and include the device you are using, what you were trying to do, and any error message you saw.

Help topics

  • Account: profile, access, settings, and account deletion requests.
  • Rewards: Bag Points, Store Credit, rewards history, and redemption help.
  • Deals: local offers, weekly ads, saved offers, and eligibility questions.
  • Scanning: checkout codes, product scans, receipt scans, and camera permission.
  • Privacy: location controls, shared content, and data questions.

App review access

Bag Guy core features can be reviewed without login. Open the app, tap Start Saving, then explore Home, Deals, Rewards, Scan, and Meal Planner.
