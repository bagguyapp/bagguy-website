✓Bag GuyPrivacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

Bag Guy helps shoppers save money on groceries, household items, and everyday essentials with local deals, rewards, scanning, meal planning, and sharing tools.

Information Bag Guy may collect

  • Location information, if enabled, to show nearby deals, retailers, and weekly ads.
  • Usage data, such as app actions and feature engagement, to improve the app experience.
  • Account information, such as email or profile details, if account features are enabled.
  • User content, such as shopping lists, recipes, shared deals, and support messages, if sharing or support features are used.

How information is used

  • To show local deals, weekly ads, retailer offers, and relevant savings.
  • To operate Bag Points, rewards history, Store Credit, and app features.
  • To improve performance, reliability, safety, and product quality.
  • To provide customer support and respond to requests.

Personal data

Bag Guy does not sell personal data. Location is used to improve local savings and is not shared with retailers as an exact personal location.
